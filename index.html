What did I learn?

Capitalisation of words means making some letters big, like A or B. We use these big letters, called capital letters.

Capitalisation Rule Examples
People's names John Smith, Mary Jane, Dr. Thomas Anderson
Places New York City, Mount Everest, Grand Canyon
Countries, nationalities, and languages Canada, Japanese, Spanish
Institutions (companies, brands, agencies, etc.) Apple Inc., Harvard University, NASA
Historical eras Industrial Revolution, Stone Age, AD (Anno Domini), BC (Before Christ)
Days, months, and holidays Monday, December, Thanksgiving Day
Monuments Taj Mahal, Statue of Liberty, Eiffel Tower
